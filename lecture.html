Coursera directs its message to communities around the world, using the key sentences "learning is the source of human progress" and "learning is a right, not a privilege". Has the strategy to "partner with the best institutions to bring the best learning to every corner of the world".

Domestika speaks directly to its audience - the creative community. Uses as key words: create | share | learn. impact/ effort matrix

Insights:

When completing a course there are no incentives to enroll in more courses

existing artistic education platforms do not link to cultural events

Most courses do not have mentoring to help students make the connection with the job market

to help clarifying our challenge, we can built this sentence:
PEOPLE GRADUATE IN...need to find a way to...NEED...because...INSIGHT

people trained in artistic fields need to find a way to find communities where they feel culturally motivated because teaching platforms do not connect with cultural events

how can we built a network of cultural offerings for students on our platform?
