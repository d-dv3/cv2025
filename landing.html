Biography

diana dóris, 44 years old, former architect.

Since my university education, I've spent several years combining my personal project as a jewelry designer with part time jobs as administrative or costumer service work.

Turning 40, I felt a great desire of change, of learning new skills in order to find a more functional and satisfatory work solution - so I decided to give myself another chance and prepare for the second part of my life!

Back to the beginning, I went back to school. I took the UX & UI Design course at edit. I particularly liked the User Experience discipline for its methodology and way of thinking centered on empathy for the user. All research phases are also very captivating, but the Design Thinking approach as a group discussion strategy was, without a doubt, one of my favorite experiences. With the User Interface discipline I learned to think better about the interaction between human beings and a digital tool or machine. And how good use of visual design can be crucial.

By exploring these paths, I realized that I could also acquire knowledge on how the “machine” works - in this case a web product - and I decided to take the Front-End Web Developer course at Flag.

Following this path of new learning, I felt the need to take another important step and left my administrative work to be able to dedicate myself fully to studying.

I learned HTML, CSS and I still study JavaScript. With more availability, I took an observation drawing course at the drawing club. After all, drawing was always the first fundamental tool, present since the beginning.

While I wait for a job opportunity, I continue to design and learn using the platforms: interaction design foundation, domestika and udemy.

Domestika speaks directly to its audience - the creative community. Uses as key words: create | share | learn.
